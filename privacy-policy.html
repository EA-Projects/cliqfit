Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/17/2025

We value your privacy and want you to feel safe using CLIQFIT. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

To make CLIQFIT the best experience possible, we collect:

  • Basic info: Your name, email, and any details you provide when signing up.
  • Fitness data: If you connect fitness trackers, we collect activity-related data to personalize your experience.
  • Device & usage info: Things like your IP address, device type, and app activity to improve performance.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your data to:

  • Improve your experience and give you accurate fitness insights.
  • Personalize your journey with CLIQFIT.
  • Send updates or promotions (you can opt-out anytime!).
  • Analyze trends and make our app even better.

3. Do We Share Your Data?

Your privacy is important to us! We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share it in certain cases:

  • With trusted service providers who help keep CLIQFIT running smoothly.
  • If required by law or necessary to protect safety and rights.
  • If CLIQFIT merges with another company (we'll notify you if that happens!).

4. How Do We Keep Your Data Safe?

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% hack-proof, so always be mindful of what you share.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You're in control! You can:

  • Update or delete your account anytime.
  • Opt-out of promotional messages if you prefer fewer emails.
  • Request access, changes, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. External Links & Third-Party Services

Sometimes, CLIQFIT may contain links to other websites or services. We can't control what they do with your data, so we recommend checking their privacy policies before using them.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we'll let you know. If you continue using CLIQFIT, it means you're okay with the changes.

8. Got Questions?

We'd love to help! If you have any privacy concerns, reach out to us at marisa@cliqfitapp.com

Final Thoughts

We created CLIQFIT to help make fitness more fun, interactive, and engaging. These policies are here to keep things fair and transparent so you can feel confident using the app.

If you ever have questions, don't hesitate to reach out! We're here to make your CLIQFIT experience amazing.
