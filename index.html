Turn Your Workouts Into Games!

CliqFit is the only fitness app that lets users compete in games powered entirely by their workouts, and this is just the beginning. With an emphasis on inclusivity and enjoyment, CliqFit helps people at all fitness levels connect through play while achieving their activity goals.

The Games

Bingo!

Each fitness Bingo board has different patterns to complete. Choose your pattern and difficulty level and then challenge yourself or a friend in solo and versus modes.

How it Works

Build Your Metrics

Your phone tracks your daily activity. When you have enough metrics for a tile, you’ll see a checkmark instead of progress!

Unlock Tiles

When you have enough metrics, tap any tile to spend them and unlock it. Work toward completing your pattern.

Challenge a Friend

Race against a friend to complete your board first! Create solo games or invite someone for friendly competition.

How it Works

Your Word Grid

Each day has a new 6-letter word to discover. Make guesses to reveal which letters are correct!

Step-Based Attempts

Your first attempt is free! Walk 2,000 steps to unlock attempt 2, 4,000 for attempt 3, and 6,000 for attempt 4.

Challenge a Friend

Race against a friend to guess the word first! Create solo games or invite someone for friendly competition.
